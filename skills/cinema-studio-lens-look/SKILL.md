# Cinema Studio Lens Look

Use this skill when the request asks for premium film image quality, cinema-studio style, lens/aperture control, or high-end CG realism.

## Default Optical Package
- 2.35:1 anamorphic widescreen intent.
- Highest practical resolution.
- 85mm focal length.
- T1.8 large aperture.
- ARRI Master Primes lens feel.
- Extreme shallow depth of field when appropriate.
- Subtle film grain.
- Bloom and halation around highlights.
- Natural motion blur following the 24fps shutter rule.
- Deep background blur with oval bokeh.
- High-end cinematic CG or film-grade live-action texture.

## Prompting Rule
- Do not use these as generic decoration. Tie lens, bokeh, light, texture, and motion blur to the shot's emotional purpose, action readability, and story tension.
